# NutriWise AI: Personalized Nutrition Planning Using Google Gemini

## The Problem

Millions of people rely on generic diet plans found online. These plans often fail to consider individual health conditions, local food availability, climate, dietary preferences, and lifestyle factors.

A person living in Bangalore with diabetes has very different nutritional needs compared to someone living in Delhi with hypertension or a person in Mumbai managing asthma. Generic recommendations often ignore these differences, making them difficult to follow and less effective.

## The Idea

To address this challenge, I built **NutriWise AI**, an AI-powered nutrition and wellness assistant that generates personalized nutrition plans based on:

* Health conditions
* City and country
* Dietary preferences
* Lifestyle information
* Climate and environmental factors

The goal is to provide practical and personalized recommendations that are easier to follow and more relevant to the user's daily life.

## Technology Used

NutriWise AI is built using:

* Google Gemini API
* Python
* Streamlit
* GitHub
* Streamlit Cloud

Google Gemini acts as the intelligence layer that analyzes user inputs and generates detailed nutrition and wellness recommendations.

## How It Works

Users enter:

* Age
* City
* Country
* Food preference
* Health conditions
* Additional lifestyle information

The system then generates:

* Daily, weekly, or monthly nutrition plans
* Recommended foods
* Foods to avoid
* Hydration guidance
* Lifestyle recommendations
* Climate-aware nutrition suggestions
* Environmental and air-quality considerations

## Key Features

### Personalized Nutrition Planning

Recommendations are tailored to the user's specific health profile.

### Multi-Condition Support

The system can consider multiple health conditions simultaneously while avoiding contradictory dietary advice.

### Climate-Aware Recommendations

Nutrition suggestions consider local weather, seasonal conditions, and environmental factors.

### Lifestyle Guidance

Users receive recommendations related to sleep, exercise, stress management, and meal timing.

## Impact

NutriWise AI demonstrates how Generative AI can be used to solve a practical everyday problem. By combining location awareness, health information, and AI reasoning, the platform provides more relevant and personalized nutrition guidance than generic online diet plans.
